## 🎤 Q&A Preparation

### Expected Questions & Answers

**Q: How accurate are the agents?**
> A: We use GPT-4 for complex reasoning and function calling for structured outputs, ensuring high accuracy. Plus, we can add human-in-the-loop for critical decisions.

**Q: What about security and compliance?**
> A: The system includes audit logging, role-based access control (ready for implementation), and follows security best practices. All actions are tracked for compliance.

**Q: How much does it cost to run?**
> A: Using GPT-3.5-turbo for most tasks keeps costs low at $0.01-0.05 per request. For 1,000 employees with 10 requests/day, that's only $300-500/month - far less than the $80K+ in labor savings.

**Q: Can it integrate with our existing systems?**
> A: Absolutely! The tool framework is designed for easy integration. We're using mock data now, but tools can connect to any API, database, or service.

**Q: How long to deploy?**
> A: The core system is ready now. Integration with your specific systems (SAP, Workday, etc.) would take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity.

**Q: What if the agent makes a mistake?**
> A: We have error handling, validation logic, and can configure human approval for critical actions. Plus, all actions are logged for audit.

**Q: How does it compare to RPA?**
> A: RPA is rigid and breaks easily. Our solution uses AI for flexibility, natural language for ease of use, and intelligent routing. It's RPA 2.0.

**Q: Can we add custom agents?**
> A: Yes! The modular architecture makes it easy. Just define the agent's role, tools, and add it to the workflow. Takes a few hours, not weeks.
